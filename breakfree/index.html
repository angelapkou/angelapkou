"Dance to Inspire"

Source: BreakFree Facebook

Founded by Laura Romeo (Class of '09) in the fall of 2006, BreakFree began as a club that offered free dance classes to the Cornell community. Her passion for dance was contagious, and BreakFree soon expanded to include a performance group that specializes in hip hop choreography. BreakFree has been featured in USA Today, the UK Daily Mail, The Huffington Post, and the Cornell Daily Sun. We continue to abide by our founding principle: The opportunity to dance should be available to everyone.

BreakFree Workshops

We host free dance workshops to anyone on campus who is interested in hip hop dance. Our workshops are offered every Saturday afternoon, and every workshop is taught by a different member of BreakFree.

We teach original choreography that the crew members choreography themselves. Occasionally, we have guest teachers from other dance crews on campus to show off a different style and provide an alternative perspective on dance. Through these workshops, we spread awareness and our love for dance to Cornell, and the greater Ithaca area.

BreakFree Hip Hop Crew

We are a family of dancers who share creativity and encourage one another to be true to ourselves. Through original choreography and our own inspirations, we are able to share with others who we are through dance.

Entry onto the crew is by audition-only, and we hold auditions at the beginning of every semester. Throughout the year, we guest perform at various campus events to entertain and inspire the Cornell community. At the end of every spring semester, we organize and headline a showcase that attracts hundreds of people. Our hope is that we can inspire others through our choreography and passion for dance. We are more than just a campus group--we are a family that lives by the motto: "dance to inspire."
