Past Events

Past Workshops

In our free weekly workshops, we offer the opportunity to anyone in the greater Ithaca area to come and dance with us. We have had guest choreographers who come and tell about their personal experiences with hip hop or any variety of dance. We have had guests such as Jason Diaz, Kyle Muna, Kenichi Kasamatsu, Bo Belza come, and countless others to come and work with our group. In addition, active members of our group are able to lead workshops to work demonstrate and share something that they have been working on.

Past Showcases

Our showcases give us an opportunity to show the campus community what BreakFree is all about. We enjoy the chance to share our most polished dances after months of hard work and light nights rehearsing. We have performed in Bailey Hall, Willard Straight Hall, and various other locations around campus. We also performed at this year's annual Fall Employee Celebration on October 26.

Watch our most recent showcase: XI Break

Past Competitions

In the past, we have travelled to different dance competitions to compete and show off our hard work. We have gone to the World of Dance Competition in Boston. Our previous two competitions were at Reign and Shine.
